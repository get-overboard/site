Automatic Surface Level Obstacle Detection

Started from UBC's New Venture Design class, Overboard strives to find new and innovative ways for safe, easy and hassle free boating. To do this, we created a unique machine learning algorithm, combined with top of the line infrared cameras, to spot objects (>17cm) up to 100m away.
